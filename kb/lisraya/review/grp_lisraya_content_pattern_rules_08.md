### Brand Voice
- **Confident, not boastful** — first-of-its-kind significance stated clearly and directly, never overstated or sensationalized.
- **Warm, not overly emotional** — genuine empathy about DM's daily impact; patients at the center.
- **Clear, not complicated** — plain, respectful language; easy to understand without losing accuracy.
